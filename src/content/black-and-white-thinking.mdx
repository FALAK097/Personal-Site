# On Black and White Thinking

Binary thinking can be both a blessing and a curse in software development. While computers operate in binary, human problems rarely have such clear-cut solutions.

## The Challenge

In software development, we often encounter situations where we need to make decisions that seem binary at first glance:

- Monolith vs. Microservices
- SQL vs. NoSQL
- Client-side vs. Server-side rendering
